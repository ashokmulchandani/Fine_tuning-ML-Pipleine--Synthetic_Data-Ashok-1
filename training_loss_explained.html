What the Model Is "Wrong" About

For instruction fine-tuning, the model sees:

Input: "### Instruction: What is Metformin? ### Response:"
Expected: "Metformin is a biguanide..."
Model guesses: "The Metrics..." ← WRONG! Loss is HIGH

After training...
Model guesses: "Metformin is a biguanide..." ← CLOSE! Loss is LOW

At EVERY position of EVERY word, the model predicts what comes next. Loss = average of how wrong it was across all positions.
